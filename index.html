Featured Recipe

Barbecue Mac-N-Cheese

Ingredients

  • Boston Butt
  • Your choice of Rub
  • Your choice of Barbecue Sauce (My favorite is Sweet Baby Rays)

Directions

Rinse the butt and pat dry. Put your choice of a rub and let sit in the fridge up to 24 hours.

Put in a crock-pot on low heat for 8-12 hours depending on the size of your butt.
